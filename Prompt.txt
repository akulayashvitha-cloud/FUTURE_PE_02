Act as a senior performance marketer, UGC ad strategist, and conversion copywriter.

Your task is to create high-converting UGC-style ad content for a local business that can be used for Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, and paid ads.

Business Details:
- Name: GlowCare Skin Clinic
- Location: Hyderabad
- Target Audience:
  - People aged 18–40
  - Facing acne, hair fall, dull skin, or confidence issues

Primary Goals:
- Increase appointment bookings
- Build trust and credibility
- Create relatable, scroll-stopping content

----------------------------------------

1. CREATE 3 UGC VIDEO AD SCRIPTS (30–45 seconds each)

Each script must follow this structure:
- Hook (first 3 seconds – strong pattern interrupt)
- Problem (pain point)
- Struggle (emotional frustration)
- Discovery (finding the clinic)
- Solution (treatment/process)
- Result (visible improvement + confidence)
- CTA (clear action)

Make it:
- Natural and conversational
- Like a real customer speaking to camera
- Emotion-driven but not exaggerated
- Platform-ready for short-form content

----------------------------------------

2. GENERATE 7 VIRAL HOOKS

- Highly engaging
- Curiosity-driven or problem-based
- Designed to stop scrolling instantly

----------------------------------------

3. GENERATE 7 HIGH-CONVERTING CTA LINES

- Create urgency
- Encourage immediate action
- Focus on booking, consultation, or visiting

----------------------------------------

4. GENERATE 7 SOCIAL MEDIA CAPTIONS

- Short and engaging
- Include emotional + benefit-driven messaging
- Suitable for Instagram / YouTube
- Optional light use of emojis

----------------------------------------

5. GENERATE 3 VIDEO SHOOT IDEAS

For each idea include:
- Scene description
- Camera angle (selfie / mirror / clinic)
- Mood (casual / emotional / confident)

----------------------------------------

TONE & STYLE GUIDELINES:
- Human-like, authentic, relatable
- Avoid robotic or generic phrases
- Focus on transformation and confidence
- Use simple, clear language

----------------------------------------

OUTPUT FORMAT:
- Clearly structured with headings
- Ready to use for real marketing campaigns
- High-conversion focused
